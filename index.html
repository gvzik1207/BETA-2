合氣道 (Aikido Club)

Club Adviser – Gelkoff A. Calmerin

The Tay Tung Aikido Club is a martial arts club inspired by the philosophy of Harmony. Aikido directly translates as The Way of Harmony.

Rules

Attend all scheduled training unless with a valid excuse.
Always observe proper hygiene.
Always clean and take care of Dojo (Training hall), training equipment, and uniform.
Respect the Sensei, classmates, and guests.
Be responsible for personal development.
Help others develop themselves.

Programs

Keiko (basic training), Zazen (meditation), Ukemi (breakfall), Ken-Jo Suburi (sword and staff training), Randori (freestyle), and more.

Armonia Music Club

Club Adviser – Harold Christian G. Montoya

The Armonia Music Club provides student musicians a space to explore and enhance their musical talents. "Armonia" means harmony, reflecting the club’s goal of nurturing musical skill, teamwork, and emotional expression.

Rules

Attend all rehearsals and activities unless excused.
Maintain instruments and materials.
Respect the adviser and members.
Be punctual and prepared.
Practice regularly.

Programs

Music Creation, Rehearsals, Performance Engagements, Musical Collaboration, and Workshops.

Book Lovers Club

Club Advisers – Mrs. Mary Jane M. Zarceno, Mrs. Yulita Q. Suarez

A club for readers and creatives who love discussing books and making crafts. Members exchange ideas, share stories, and develop creativity.

Rules

Be respectful, take turns, be kind.
Handle books with care.
Come prepared and participate.
Have fun and be creative.

Programs

Read-Aloud Sessions, Book Talks, Silent Reading Time, Book-Themed Crafts, Story Retelling, and Puppet Making.

CHEFS (Creative Hands Exploring Foods and Sweets)

Club Advisers – Mrs. Angela D. Dumay, LPT; Ms. Ma. Catherine B. Llanto, LPT

The CHEFS Club lets young learners explore baking while learning patience, creativity, and life skills.

Rules

Attend all meetings and sessions.
Maintain cleanliness.
Be respectful.
Share tools responsibly.
Follow safety procedures.

Programs

Weekly Baking Sessions, Sweet Treats Day, Inter-grade Bake-off, Guest Demos, and Holiday-Themed Baking.
