合氣道 (Aikido Club)

Club Adviser – Gelkoff A. Calmerin

The Tay Tung Aikido Club is a martial arts club inspired by the philosophy of Harmony. Aikido directly translates as The Way of Harmony.

Rules

Attend all scheduled training unless with a valid excuse.
Always observe proper hygiene.
Always clean and take care of Dojo and equipment.
Respect the Sensei and classmates.
Extend discipline beyond the dojo.

Programs

Keiko (basic training), Zazen (meditation), Ukemi (breakfall), Randori (freestyle), Ken-Jo Suburi (sword and staff training).
