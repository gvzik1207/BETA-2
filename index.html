The Tay Tung Aikido Club is a martial arts club inspired by the philosophy of Harmony. Aikido directly translates as The Way of Harmony.

Rules
Attend all scheduled training unless with a valid excuse. Always observe proper hygiene. Always clean and take care of Dojo, equipment, and uniform. Respect everyone. Extend discipline beyond the Dojo.

Programs
Keiko (basic training), Zazen (meditation), Ashi sabaki (footwork), Ukemi (breakfall), Tachi waza (standing techniques), Suwari waza (seated techniques), Hanmi handachi waza (seated vs standing), Randori (freestyle), Ken-Jo Suburi and Kata (sword/staff training), Kyu exams, and Martial Arts Seminars.
